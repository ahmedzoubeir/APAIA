**Question 1**

**Premise:** According to MITRE ATT&CK, which technique ID and name is related to the absence of proper source validation?

**Options:**
A. T1114: Origin Validation Error
B. T1190: Exploit Public-Facing Application
C. T1214: Remote Desktop Protocol Brute Force
D. T1496: Schedule Task/Job

**Correct Answer:** A
**Explanation:** T1114: Origin Validation Error is the correct answer as it pertains to the MITRE ATT&CK technique of failing to validate the source of data or communication.

**Question 2**

**Premise:** An organization aims to incorporate recommendations from the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF). Which CSF component focuses on assessing and managing cybersecurity risks?

**Options:**
A. Identify
B. Protect
C. Detect
D. Respond

**Correct Answer:** C
**Explanation:** Detect is the correct answer as it represents the CSF component responsible for assessing and managing cybersecurity risks through continuous monitoring and detection mechanisms.

**Question 3**

**Premise:** Which NIST publication provides guidance for integrating cybersecurity and enterprise risk management (ERM)?

**Options:**
A. NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
B. Integrating Cybersecurity and Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)
C. Risk Management Framework (RMF)
D. Privacy Framework

**Correct Answer:** B
**Explanation:** Integrating Cybersecurity and Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) is the correct answer as it is the NIST publication that provides guidelines for integrating cybersecurity risks into an organization's ERM program.

**Question 4**

**Premise:** The NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF) includes a provision for incorporating privacy risk management. Which component of RMF facilitates this integration?

**Options:**
A. Risk Assessment
B. Risk Response
C. System Security Plan
D. Continuous Monitoring

**Correct Answer:** A
**Explanation:** Risk Assessment is the correct answer because it involves identifying and analyzing privacy risks, which is a key aspect of incorporating privacy risk management into RMF.

**Question 5**

**Premise:** An organization is developing a Workforce Framework for Cybersecurity (NICE Framework) to enhance its cybersecurity posture. Which dimension of the NICE Framework describes the knowledge and skills required for performing cybersecurity tasks?

**Options:**
A. Task Level
B. Knowledge Level
C. Skill Level
D. Experience Level

**Correct Answer:** B
**Explanation:** Knowledge Level is the correct answer as it represents the dimension of the NICE Framework that focuses on the knowledge and skills required to perform specific cybersecurity tasks.
